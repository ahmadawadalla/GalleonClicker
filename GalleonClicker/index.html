The game starts out with the player having no galleons. The player has the option to click the Big Coin, on the left side of the screen, which produces one galleon per click.

When the player has 15 galleons, they may buy a muggle, which makes you 1 galleon every 10 seconds. The player can then keep producing galleons, until they buy goblins, dobbys, hagrids and so on. There is also more information on each person when you hover over them.
